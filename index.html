Massage

Indulge your mind, body and soul with our healing music, herbal teas and essential oils while choosing one of our Ayurvedic and Aroma-theraputic DoTerra oil massages

At senses our ethos is to bring nourishment, protection and concious well-being to both you and our beautiful planet

All of our ingredients come from the highest quality of natural, organic, fair-trade, vegan and cruelty free resources and our spa is sustainable which helps reduce energy, water and waste.

Treatments

Offering you an exclusive range of natural and organic treatments, using the latest technologies designed to reduce energy, water and waste. Protecting both you and our wonderful planet

Senses is eco-sustainable to help our planet. We use biodegradable bags,towels and gowns and energy-saving hairdryers, heaters and lightbulbs

Our electrity is from 'green energy' suppliers and , we joined 'green salon collective' hair trimmings are recycled as mats for oil spills.

We care about you and the environment

We use Oway hair colours, perms, styling and beauty products.These are biodynamic, organic, vegan, cruelty-free, ethical, and FairTrade. The highest quality ingredients are infused with essential oils and contain no ammonia, synthetic fragrance, parabens, SLS, SLES, PPG's or PEG's. We keep it "clean and green" as what goes onto your-scalp is absorbed into the bloodstream in 20 seconds and also washed into our water systems! Oway packaging is reusable,refillable,recyclable and plastic-free. Precious Amber glass, sustainable wood, resin, clay or aluminium is used.
